Mobile Randomized Controlled Trial Tool

RCTrials is an open source tool to help researchers collect and track survey data and carry out randomized controlled trials. It is mobile, has an API, and is available on GitHub.

Participating

in a trial?

Download the Surveyor app
and register into your trial.

Doing Research

using a RCT?

Create an account to start configuring your study.

an app?
